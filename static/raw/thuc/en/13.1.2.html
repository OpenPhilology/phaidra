DIRECTIONS FOR READING

  • 1. Read each sentence aloud in the original. Pronounce each word distinctly.
  • In reading,
    1. a. Observe sharply the forms of the words, so as to become at once aware of their grammatical relations.
    2. b. Make the utmost effort of memory to recall the meanings of words already met.
    3. c. Follow the Greek order strictly in arriving at the thought. Observe carefully the order of the words and the marks of punctuation.
  • 2. If the thought expressed in the sentence is not perfectly clear, repeat the whole process.
  • 3. Translate the sentence into simple, idiomatic language.
