The genitive plural of nouns of this declension always has the circumflex on the last syllable.

For the accent of the other forms, see 35, 22 (final αι is short, 23), 36. The forms ἡ, αἱ of the article are proclitic (26). The vocative of the article does not occur.

The nominative and vocative of these nouns, both singular and plural, are alike (30).
