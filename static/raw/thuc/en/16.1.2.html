Notes on the Formation of the Imperfect, Aorist, and Perfect Active Indicative

The imperfect is formed on the augmented present stem .

The first aorist stem of ἄγω is λῡσα, formed by adding the tense suffix σα to the verb stem. The aorist has augment. In the first person singular, ν is dropped, and in the third α is changed to ε.

The first pluperfect is formed on the first perfect stem, with κε for κα. In the singular ν is dropped and κε appears as κη, κη, κει.
