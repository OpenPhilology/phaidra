VOCABULARY

καί conj., and, also.
κακή adj., bad.
καλή adj. beautiful, fine.
γνώμη, ης, ἡ means of knowing, mark, token, judgement will, opinion, mind, intelligence.
μάχη, ης, ἡ battle, fight.
σπονδή, ης, ἡ drink-offering, libation, treaty.
ἀρχή, ης, ἡ beginning.
παρασκευή, ης, ἡ preparation.
Ἀθήνη, ης, ἡ Athens.
φυλακή, ῆς, ἡ watch, guard, garrison.
φοβερά adj., frightful, fearful.
