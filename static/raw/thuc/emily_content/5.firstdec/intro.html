First declension nouns are often called α- declension nouns...because most them have an α- ending!

First Declension

Now that you have learned how to identify the 3 main characteristics of Greek nouns, how to distinguish the 5 Greek cases and understand the role that cases individually play in a sentence, you are ready for your first declension!

In this Section

After completing "The First or α- Declension", you will learn to recognize and translate many commonly used Greek nouns.

Story
You'll read about the events that take place at the very beginning of the Pentecontaetia.
Skills
You'll learn how to:
  1. Identify first declension nouns ending in -α.
  2. Identify first declension nouns ending in -η.
  3. Identify first declension nouns ending in -ης.
Vocabulary
You'll learn some of the most common Greek first declension nouns that appear in the Pentecontaetia.
