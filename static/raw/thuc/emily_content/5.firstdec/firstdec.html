First declension

The first Greek declension consists of nearly exclusively feminine nouns. Only few masculine nouns belong to the first declension and no neuter nouns. The first and most common group of nouns ends in -α in the whole singular and plural. Take a look at the declension of ἡ χώρα meaning the land.
