The First Declension in the Pentecontaetia

The following is an example of how Thucydides himself makes use of first declension nouns in the Pentecontaetia.

[...] οἱ βάρβαροι ἐκτῆς χώρας ἀπῆλθον
[...] the barbarians from the country departed
(Nominative) (Genitive) (Verb)
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.89.3

τῆς χώρας belongs to the first group of first declension nouns. It is feminine, singular and in the genitive case!
