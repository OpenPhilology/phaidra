Welcome to Greek with Thucydides!

In your journey to learn the basics of the Ancient Greek language you will be guided by Thucydides, who is best known as the author of the renowned History of the Peloponnesian War. You'll learn from examples in Greek from this famous historical work, contextualized by snippets of his account in English. By the end of this course, you will feel comfortable reading Thucydides' in Ancient Greek!

In this Section

After completing "Introduction to Nouns", you'll understand many important fundamentals of the Ancient Greek language.

Story
You'll learn who Thucydides was, how he got involved in the Peloponnesian War, and the overall signifiance of the war in ancient history.
Skills
You'll learn how to:
  1. Identify the Gender, Number, and Case of a Greek Noun.
  2. Figure out the noun's role in a sentence.
Vocabulary
You'll learn some of the most useful nouns found in Thucydides' writing!
