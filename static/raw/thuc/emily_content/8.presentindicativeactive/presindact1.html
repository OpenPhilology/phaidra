Present Indicative Active, λύω - To untie
Person
Singular
1st λύω I unbtie
2nd λύεις You untie
3rd λύει He/She/It unties
Plural
1st λύομεν We untie
2nd λύετε You untie
3rd λύουσι(ν) They untie

Present Indicative Active

To form the present indicative active, identify the present tense stem by removing the variable ending - ω. Add the endings from the table to indicate person and number.

Keep and eye out!
The letter ν at the end of the third person plural is called nu-movable. It may be added to certain endings when/if the following word begins with a vowel or at the end of a sentence.
