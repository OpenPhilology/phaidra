Eta

Η η

Say it like the "e" in echo
IPA [ɛː] Listen

Eta is pronounced the same in ἦχος (échos), meaning "sound".
Think of echo, echolocation, and Echo (the forest nymph punished by Hera to repeat only what other people said).
