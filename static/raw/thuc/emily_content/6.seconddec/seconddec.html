Second declension

Second declension nouns either have the nominative singular ending in -ος for masculine and, less frequently, feminine nouns, or the ending in -ον for the neuter. Here is the declension for the Greek word ὁ λόγος meaning the word.

The Genitive is the key!

Remember that to decline you must take the genitive singular, remove the ending to get the stem and add the correct ending to form the other cases.
