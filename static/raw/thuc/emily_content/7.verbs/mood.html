Mood

The mood of a verb indicates whether a sentence is a factual statement, hypothetical, wishful, a commanding statement and so on. In Greek there are four moods:

  • Indicative: mood for factual statements and questions. For example I am a soldier
  • Subjunctive: mood for hypothetical and other types of statements. For example If I were a soldierâ€¦
  • Optative: mood for conditional statements. For example I may be a soldier
  • Imperative: mood for commanding statements. For example Be a soldier!
