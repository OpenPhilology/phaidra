Let's take a closer look at a verb's characteristics.

Person

"Person" describes who is performing the action of the verb.

  • If the subject of the verb is I or we, the verb is in the first person.
  • If the subject is you, the verb is in the second person.
  • If the subject is he/she/it or they, the verb is in the third person.
