Voice

Voice defines how the subject of the verb is involved in the action of the verb.

  • If the subject is performing the action, the voice is active. For example I scare.
  • If the subject is receiving the action from an outside agency, the voice is passive. For example I am scared byâ€¦
  • If the subject is performing the action with personal involvement, the voice is middle. For example I am frightened.
