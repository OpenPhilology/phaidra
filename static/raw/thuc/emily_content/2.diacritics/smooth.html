Smooth Breathing

When words begin with a vowel, but don't have a rough breathing, then you will see this sign ᾿ on the first letter. This sign ᾿ is called smooth breathing. Smooth breathings have no effect on pronunciation.

Useful tip!
The majority of words beginning with a vowel will have a smooth breathing.
