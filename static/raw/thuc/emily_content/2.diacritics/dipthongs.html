Dipthongs

Dipthongs are vowels in pairs of two that produce one long sound. Words that begin with a dipthong will have either a rough or smooth breathing (depending on the word). Whichever breathing it is, it is placed on the second vowel of the dipthong. For example: αἰτία, cause.

All dipthongs are counted as long.
