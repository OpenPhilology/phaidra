Long and Short Vowels

The Ancient Greeks pronounced vowels differently according to their "length". The length of a vowel, therefore, has an effect on the accent rules that you will be learning later in this unit. For now, all that you need to know is that vowels can be divided into two categories:

  • Long vowels
  • Short vowels

Some vowels can be both long or short depending on the word. Take a look at the table to learn which ones.

Notice how η is simply the long version of ε, and ω is the long version of ο.
