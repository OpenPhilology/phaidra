For example:

ὁδός (hodos), Way



Or:

῾Ελληνες (Hellenes), Greeks

Rough Breathing

When you see this symbol ῾ on top of the first letter of a word beginning with a vowel, the word is pronounced as though the first letter were preceded by an "H". This symbol ῾ is called rough breathing.

Useful tip!
All words starting with the vowel υ have a rough breathing. All words starting with ρ also have a rough breathing, but in this case, as ρ is a consonant and not a vowel, the breathing has no effect on how the word sounds.
