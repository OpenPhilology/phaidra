Two-ending Adjectives of the Second Declension

As you can see from the table, the adjective ἄδικος, ἄδικον has the same endings as second declension nouns.

Example: τοῦ ἀδίκου ἀνθρώπου, meaning of the unjust man.

Meet the Alpha Privative

The adjective ἄδικος, meaning unjust is the exact opposite in meaning of δίκαιος meaning just. The clue is in the prefix α, also called alpha privative, expressesing negation or absence.
