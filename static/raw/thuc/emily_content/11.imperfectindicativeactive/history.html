The Spartans and the Athenians are no longer friends...but were they ever?

...previously in the Pentecontaetia

The Athenians grew less popular by demanding monetary and military contributions from the alliance.

Now in the Pentecontaetia...

The Spantans, finding it difficult to supress the revolt of the Helots on their own, call for Athenian help. Cimon and his troups are sent to help Sparta, but, upon their arrival, they are rudely dismissed by οἱ Λακεδαιμόνιοι, the Spartans. This is because the Spartans are suddenly afraid that the Athenians, after having helped, might demand political changes. The Athenians take offense at this act and decide to renounce their alliance with Sparta in favor for one with Argos, Sparta's enemy!

(Summary of Thuc. 1.102)
