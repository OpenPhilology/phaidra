The Dative Case

Datives in the Pentecontaetia

In this section right at the beginning of the Pentecontaetia, Thucydides talks about king Leotychides' victory at Mycale in 479BC, when he gave the Persians the final blow.

Λεωτυχίδης ἡγεῖτο τῶν Ἑλλήνων ἐν Μυκάλῃ...
Leotychides led the Greeks at Mycale...
(Nominative) (Verb - ἡγέομαι takes an object in the genitive) (Genitive) (Dative)  
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.89.2
