The Accusative Case

Accusative in the Pentecontaetia!

In this section, Thucydides is describing the Athenians taking over the city of Sestus. When they were sure that the barbarians were gone for good, the soldiers went back for their families.

Ἀθηναίων δὲ τὸ κοινόν [...] ὅθεν ὑπεξέθεντο παῖδας καὶ γυναῖκας.
The people of Athens brought over children and women.
(Subject) (Verb) (Direct Objects)
— The Peloponnesian War, 1.89.3
