The Dative Case

Dative meaning in

In these sentences, the dative expresses metaphorical or physical location. Thucydides often uses the dative in this way. (When indicating a physical location, the dative is often preceeded by the preposition ἐν.)

ὁ ἀνήρ ἐστὶ νέος ἡλικίᾳ.
The man is young in age.
(Nominative) (Verb) (Complement) (Dative)
Genitive meaning with or by

In these sentences, the dative expresses means or instrument.Thucydides often uses the dative in this way.

ἡ κόρη βάλλει τόν ἄνδρα λίθοις
The girl hits the man with stones.
(Nominative) (Verb) (Accusative) (Dative)
