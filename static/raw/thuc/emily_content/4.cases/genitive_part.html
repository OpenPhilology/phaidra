The Genitive Case

Partitive Genitive

In these sentences, the genitive indicates the part of something.

ἡ συντυχία τῆς ἱστορίας ἐστὶ βραχεῖα.
The event of (out of) the narrative is short.
(Nominative) (Genitive) (Verb)
Genitive of Separation

In these sentences, the genitive indicates the separation or lack of something.

ἡ κόρη ἀφίσταται τῶν κινδύνων.
The girl stays away from the dangers
(Nominative) (Verb) (Genitive)
Genitive with verbs of Desire

In these sentences, the genitive follows verbs of desire.

ὁ Θουκυδίδης ἐπιθυμέι τῆς ἡσυχίας.
Thucydides desires rest.
