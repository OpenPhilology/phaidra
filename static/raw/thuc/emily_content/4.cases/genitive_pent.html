The Genitive Case

Genitives in the Pentecontaetia

In this section right at the beginning of the Pentecontaetia, the Persians have just suffered the final defeat by the Greeks, or Hellenes, led by the Spartan Leotychides.

Λεωτυχίδης μὲν ὁ βασιλεὺς τῶν Λακεδαιμονίων ἀπεχώρησεν ἐπ᾽ οἴκου
Leotychides, king of the Spartans set out towards home...
(Nominative) (Genitive) (Verb)  
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.89.2
