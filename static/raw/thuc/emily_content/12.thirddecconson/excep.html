A few things to remember!

When the final consonant of a stem meets the dative plural ending -σι , it causes the following spelling changes:

Consonants Dative Ending Results in Example
π, β, φ + -σι = -ψι angelos
κ, γ, χ + -σι = -ξι anküra
τ, δ, θ + -σι = λόγχη lonkhe
ν + -σι = σάλπιγξ salpinx
ν + -σι = σάλπιγξ salpinx
The consonants are grouped according to sound.
