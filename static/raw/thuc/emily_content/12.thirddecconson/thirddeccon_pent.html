The Third Declension in the Pentecontaetia

The following is an example of how Thucydides himself makes use of third declension nouns in the Pentecontaetia.

οἰ [...] Ἀθηναῖοι τρόπῳ τοιῷδε ἧλθον ἐπὶ τὰ πράγματα
The [...] Athenians in this way came to the circumstances...
(Nominative)   (Verb)
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.89.1
Keep Reading »

τὰ πράγματα is a neuter, accusative plural of the noun τό πρᾶγμα.
