Definite Articles

Greek has articles that in meaning are roughly the quivalent ofthe English "the". The Greek article takes on different form for the different genders, numbers and cases. Articles always agree with the nouns they are referred to, meaning that they take the same gender, number and case of the noun.

Useful tip!
Note the change in accent from an acute to a circumflex in the genitive and dative, both singular and plural.
