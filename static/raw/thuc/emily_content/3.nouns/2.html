Number

Just like it is in English, every Greek noun is either singular or plural. As you learnt earlier, the best way of determining the number of a noun is to look at its definite article and ending.

Notice how the definite article and the ending change from singular to plural, while the stem remains unchanged:

Feminine
Singular Plural
ἡ ἡμέρα
the day 		αἱ ἡμέραι
the days
Masculine
Singular Plural
ὁ πόλεμος
the war 		οἱ πόλεμοι
the wars
Neuter
Singular Plural
τό ἔργον
the work 		τά ἔργα
the works
