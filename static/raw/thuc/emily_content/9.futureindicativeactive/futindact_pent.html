Future Indicative Active, λύσω - I will untie
Person
Singular
1st λύσω I will untie
2nd λύσεις You will untie
3rd λύσει He/She/It will untie
Plural
1st λύσομεν We will untie
2nd λύσετε You will untie
3rd λύσουσι(ν) They will untie

The First Indicative Active in the Pentecontaetia

The following is an example of how Thucydides himself makes use of a future indicative active verb in the Pentecontaetia.

οἱ ᾿Αθηναῖοι [...] πέμψουσιν πρέσβεις [...]
The Athenians [...] will send ambassadors [...]
(Nominative) (Verb)   (Object)
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.90.3
