Future Indicative Active, λύσω - I will untie
Person
Singular
1st λύσω I will untie
2nd λύσεις You will untie
3rd λύσει He/She/It will untie
Plural
1st λύσομεν We will untie
2nd λύσετε You will untie
3rd λύσουσι(ν) They will untie

Future Indicative Active

To form the future indicative active, identify the future tense stem by removing the variable ending - ω. Add the endings from the table to indicate person and number.

For example:
λύσ
ω
Stem
The stem is formed by taking the present tense stem λύ- and adding σ. 		Ending
The endings of the future indicative active are the same as those of the present indicative active.
