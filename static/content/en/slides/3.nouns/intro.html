Introduction to Thucydides and Nouns, naturally

In your journey to learn the basics of the Ancient Greek language you will be guided by me, Thucydides, best known as the author of the renowned History of the Peloponnesian War. You'll learn from examples in Greek from this famous historical work, contextualized by snippets of my account in English. By the end of this course, you will feel comfortable reading Thucydides in Ancient Greek!
