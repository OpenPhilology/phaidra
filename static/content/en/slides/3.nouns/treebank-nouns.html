Building Parse Trees for Nouns

What's a parse tree?

A parse tree is a way to represent that you understand the grammar of a greek sentence. Take a look at the example to the right:

οἱ Ἀθηναῖοι

Since οἱ ("the") depends on and agrees with (in case, number, and gender) Ἀθηναῖοι ("Athenians"), it belongs under it on the parse tree.
