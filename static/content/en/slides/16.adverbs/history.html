The Athenians win Sicyon and lose Acarnia.

...previously in the Pentecontaetia

The Athenians were forced to leave Egypt after six years of war.

Now in the Pentecontaetia...

Orestes, son of the Thessalian king, wants to be restored as master of Thessaly. To this end, he persuades the Athenians to march on Thessaly and bend the inhabitants to their will. However, the Athenians fail and Orestes is not restored!

Under the command of Pericles, the Athenians now land on Sicyon and conquer it. They fail, however, to win over Acarnia.

(Summary of Thuc. 1.111)
