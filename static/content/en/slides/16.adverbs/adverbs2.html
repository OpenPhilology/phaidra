Adverbs

There is another kind of adverb formed by the accusative neuter (singular or plural) of adjectives. Take a look at some examples below.

Adjective Adverb
ὀξύς (acute) ὀξύ (acutely)
πολύς (much) πολύ/πολλά (much)
μέγας (big) μέγα/μεγάλα (largely)

Don't leave yet! There is one last type of adverb formed with the accusative and dative of feminine adjectives or nouns. Take a look at some examples below.

Noun/Adjective Adverb
ἡ χάρις (the grace) χάριν (thanks to)
τὸ τέλος (the end) τέλος (finally)
ἡ ἀρχή (the beginning) ἀρχήν (initially)
ἴδιος (private) ἰδίᾳ (privately)
δημόσιος (public) δεμοσίᾳ (publicly)
πεζός (walking) πεζῇ (on foot)
