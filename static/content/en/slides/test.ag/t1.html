Build your own parse tree! A parse tree is a way to show that you understand the grammar and function of a Greek sentence. The root is the starting point on which you build the tree. All you have to do it figure out which words in the sentence "depend" on other words. Take the English sentence: The man eats an apple. The action verb of the sentences is eats, while the subject is man. The object is apple. Therefore, man and apple depend on eats. The article The depends on the word man, so in a tree it would be placed under it.

Now, you give it a go!     1) Click a word     2) Click the word it depends on. Simple as that!
