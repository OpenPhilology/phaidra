Are you wondering what those words above the circles mean? They indicate the relations between words. It may help you to become familiar with just a few of these. Pred describes the predicate - the verb of the sentence (for example, watch). Subj is the subject: it depends on the Pred and often describes who/what performs the action of the sentence (for example, gods). Obj is the object: it depends on the Pred and represents the element in the sentence that is being acted upon (for example, men). And Atr describes the attribute, or a descriptive element in the sentence, which could be an article or adjective that depends on a noun (for example, the or all). It's that easy!
