Future Indicative Active

To form the future indicative active:

  1. Take the present tense form, also known as the first "principle part".
    e.g. λύω
  2. Remove the ending (-ω) to get the the present stem.
    e.g. λύ-
  3. Add a σ to get the future stem.
    e.g. λύσ-
  4. Add the endings from the table to indicate the person and number.
    e.g. λύσομεν, We will untie — or — λύσεις, You will untie

The future then becomes the second principle part of a verb (while the present is the first principle part).
