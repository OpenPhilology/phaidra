Future Active Indicative — πέμψω, I will send
Person Singular Plural
1st πέμψω πέμψομεν
2nd πέμψεις πέμψετε
3rd πέμψει πέμψουσι(ν)

Future Indicative Active for Stop Verbs

The future stem of many verbs simply ends in σ, as you just learned.

But sometimes, when adding the σ to the end, it combines with the final letter of the present stem to form another letter. Take a look at the three categories below:



κ or γ or χ + σ ξ

For example:

λέγω, I say

λέξω, I will say

π or β or φ + σ ψ

For example:

πέμπω, I send

πέμψω, I will send

τ or δ or θ + σ σ

For example:

πείθω, I persuade

πείσω, I will persuade
