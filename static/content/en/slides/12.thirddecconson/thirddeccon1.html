τό ἔργον,ου: The body, of the body

Singular Plural
Nom. τό σῶμα τά σώματα
Gen. τοῦ σῶματος τῶν σωμάτων
Dat. τῷ σώματι τοῖς σώμασι(ν)
Acc. τό σῶμα τά σῶματα
Voc. ῷ σῶμα ῷ σῶματα

Third declension continued...

And now here is an example of a neuter third declension noun: τό σῶμα meaning the body.

Neuter Nouns are a Special Case

In all neuter nouns, the accusative and the vocative are the same as the nominative. This works for both singular and plural.
