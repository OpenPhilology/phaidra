Tau



Τ τ

Say it like the "t" in toxic.   IPA [t]

Tau makes the same sound in τόξον (tókson), meaning "archer's bow, rainbow, arc", and τοξικόν (toxikon) meaning "poison".
Think of toxicology, detox, intoxication.
