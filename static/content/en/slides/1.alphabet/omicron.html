Omicron

Ο ο

Say it like the "o" in optical.   IPA /o/

Omicron makes the same sound in ὀπτικός (optikós), meaning "relating to sight or vision" and in ὀφθαλμός, meaning "eye".
Think of fiberoptics, optometry, ophthalmology (medicine dealing with anatomy, physiology, and diseases of the eye).
