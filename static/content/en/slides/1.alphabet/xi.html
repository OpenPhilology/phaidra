Xi

Ξ ξ

Say it like the "ks" in cakes.   IPA /ks/

Xi makes the same sound in ξένος (ksénos), meaning "guest" or "stranger".
Think of xenophobia (literally, fear of strangers or foreigners), xenon (chemical element).
