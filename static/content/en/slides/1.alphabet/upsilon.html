Upsilon

Υ υ

Say it like the "ü" in über.   IPA [y]

An upsilon at the beginning of a word is often pronounced "hü". For example, ὕδωρ (húdōr), meaning "water"
Think of hydrology, hydrolysis (literally, loosening water).
