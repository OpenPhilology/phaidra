The Second Declension in the Pentecontaetia

The following is an example of how Thucydides himself makes use of second declension nouns in the Pentecontaetia.

[...] ὁ Θεμιστοκλῆς τοῖς Ἀθηναίοις κρύφα πέμπει [...]
[...] Themistocles to the Athenians secretly sent [...]
(Nominative) (Dative)   (Verb)
— The History of the Peloponnesian War, 1.91.3

τοῖς Ἀθηναίοις is a masculine, plural second declension noun in the dative case.
