As you can see from the table, the adjective πᾶς, πᾶσα, πᾶν has forms of the first and third declensions.

Useful tip

The adjective πᾶς, πᾶσα, πᾶν often also means all or whole.

Another adjective that follows this pattern is μέλας, μέλαινα, μέλαν, meaning black. If you look closely the endings for this particular adjective, though similar, are somewhat different from those in πᾶς, πᾶσα, πᾶν.

The very last type of adjectives following this pattern is ἡδύς, ἡδεῖα, ἡδύ, meaning sweet.
