Pluperfect Indicative Active

To form the pluperfect indicative active:

  1. Take the fourth principle part (the one for the perfect tense).
    e.g. λέλυκα
  2. Remove the ending (-α) to get the the perfect stem.
    e.g. λέλυκ-
    3. Add the augment ἐ.
    e.g. ἐλέλυκ-
  3. Add the the endings you see in the table.
    e.g. ἐλελύκειν, meaning I had untied.
