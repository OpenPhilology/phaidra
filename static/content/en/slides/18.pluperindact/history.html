...previously in the Pentecontaetia

General Cimon died in Cyprus after the Athenians conquer Citium.

Now in the Pentecontaetia...

The Boeotians regain independence from Athens after a battle at Coronea, in which many Athenians are killed and taken captive.

Soon after Euboea and Megara also revolt against the Athenians. Though managing to squash the Euboeian revolt, Pericles struggles to calm Megara.

The Peloponnesian army also invades Attica, but soon turns back!

(Summary of Thuc. 1.113-114)
