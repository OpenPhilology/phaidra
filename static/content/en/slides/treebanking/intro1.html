Building Parse Trees for Nouns

Let's get started, shall we?

It all starts with the root. The root is the starting point on which you build the tree.

When you have taken a good look at the sentence you are working on, you must identify the trunk of your tree. This is called the predicate (PRED). The PRED is a word in the sentence that does not depend on any other word and around which the sentence revolves. The PRED is the verb of a sentence, as it shapes the form of the main constituents in the sentence.
