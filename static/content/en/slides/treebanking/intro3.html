Building Parse Trees for Nouns

What's the next step?

Nouns can often be found in the company of adjectives that describe them, determiners (or definite articles) that delimit them, relative clauses and prepositional phrases that make their meaning more specific. These descriptive elements take the name of attribute (ATR). It helps to imagine them as the leaves on the branches.
