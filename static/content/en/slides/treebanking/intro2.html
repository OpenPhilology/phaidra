Building Parse Trees for Nouns

What's the next step?

Then, you can start giving your tree branches. A particularly important branch is the subject (SUBJ) of the sentence. The subject depends on the PRED and often describes who/what performs the action of the sentence. The SUBJ is often a noun.

The subject is usually in the nominative case, but you will learn that in a few other constructions it can also be in the genitive or accusative case.
