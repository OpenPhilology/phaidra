Third Accent Rule: Grave Accent

  1. The grave accent appears only on the ultima.
  2. The acute accent turns into a grave accent on the ultima when the word is not followed by a punctuation mark, but by another word.

πολλὰ κακά meaning "many evil (things)"

πολλά
Changes its accent when not followed by a punctuation mark, like so:
πολλὰ κακά.
