Fourth Accent Rule: Circumflex Accent

  1. The circumflex accent can only be placed on the penult or ultima.
  2. The circumflex accent can only be placed on long vowels or dipthongs (You will learn about dipthongs shortly).
For example:

σῶμα meaning "Body"

σῶ
μα
Penult
 Ultima

The circumflex accent is placed on the penult. The penult is a long syllable because it contains the long vowel, ῶ, omega.
