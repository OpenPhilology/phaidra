Greek Diacritics and Dipthongs
Learn the diacritics and dipthongs to read and write as the Greeks did!

Now that you have learned the Greek alphabet, it's time for you to become familiar with the Greek diacritics and dipthongs.

Diacritics are signs or marks attached to a letter that distinguish it from another of similar form, giving it a different phonetic value. Diacritics give Ancient Greek its own music!

Dipthongs indicate two vowels that, when next to each other, are pronounced as one.
