Accents

In the Greek language, accents are very important and should be learned as an intergral part of its spelling. Accents determine the music of a word and indicate which vowels in the word should be emphasized. When pronouncing a Greek word, simply stress the vowel on which you see an accent! Take a look at the table to learn what the three Greek accents are.

To help you with the spelling of Greek words, you should know that the accents follow four very specific sets of rules.
