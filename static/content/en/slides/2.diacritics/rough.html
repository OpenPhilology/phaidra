Rough Breathing

When you see this sign ῾ on the first letter of a word beginning with a vowel, you are looking at a rough breathing. The Greeks used it to indicate that the word would be pronounced as though the first letter were preceded by an "H".

Useful tip!
All words starting with the vowel υ have a rough breathing. All words starting with ρ also have a rough breathing, but in this case, as ρ is a consonant and not a vowel, the breathing has no effect on how the word sounds.
