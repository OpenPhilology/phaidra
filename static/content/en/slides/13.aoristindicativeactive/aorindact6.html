Aorist Indicative Active

The following is a list of verbs with their corresponding aorists that can be often found in Ancient Greek texts. Notice how all of these have second aorists. Try learning them!

1st: Present 2nd: Future 3rd: Aorist
ἄγω (I lead) ἄξω (I will lead) ἤγαγον (I led)
βάλλω (I throw) βαλῶ (I will throw) ἔβαλον (I threw)
πείθω (I persuade) πείσω (I will persuade) ἔπεισα (I persuaded)
τέμνω (I cut) τεμῶ (I will cut) ἔτεμον (I cut)
