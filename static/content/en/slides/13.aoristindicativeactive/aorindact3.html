Aorist Indicative Active

The asigmatic aorist behaves in a similar way to the sigmatic aorist. You'll notice, however, that in this case it is not as easy to separate the stem. Often with the aorist, it is just a case of memorizing the third principle part of some common words.

For example, in the case of φαίνω, to show, the aorist indicative active is ἔφηνα. You'll see how here there is no sigma, but the endings remain the same as the sigmatic aorist.
