The following is a list of verbs with their corresponding aorists that can be often found in Ancient Greek texts. Notice how all of these have asigmatic aorists.

Present Aorist Note
ἀγγέλλω (I announce) ἤγγειλα The augment has changed from ε to η following a merge of α + ε
κρίνω (I judge) ἔκρινα
περαίνω (I finish) ἐπέρανα
