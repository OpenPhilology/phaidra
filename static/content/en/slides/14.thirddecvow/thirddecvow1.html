Third declension: vowel stem

There are also third declension nouns with a stems ending in dipthongs. For example:

  • ἡ ναῦς, the ship, with a stem in -αυ
  • ὁ βασιλεύς, the king, with a stem in -ευ
  • ὁ βοῦς, the buffalo, with a stem in -ου
