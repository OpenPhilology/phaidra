Boeotia

The Athenians defeat the Boeotians and become their masters.

...previously in the Pentecontaetia

The Athenians defeat the Corinthians, who return home.

Now in the Pentecontaetia...

The Athenians begin work on a long τό τεῖχος, a wall reaching the sea. They also advance to engage the Peloponnesians in Boeotia. After heavy loss on both sides, the Spartans win and return home. Later the Athenians return to Boeotia and, once they have defeated their army, they become masters of the land. Aegina also surrenders to the power of the Athenians.

(Summary of Thuc. 1.107-108)
