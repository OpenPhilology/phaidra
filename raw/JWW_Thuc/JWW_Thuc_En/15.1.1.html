Analysis of the Primary Tenses of the Indicative Active.

Stems, Suffixes and Endings

To the verb stem (54) different TENSE SUFFIXES are added to form the TENSE STEMS of the verb, as λύω, stem λῡ, present tense suffix ο or ε (for convenience written ο/ε), present tense stem λῡο or λῡε (λῡ ο/ε.

To form the complete tenses, PERSONAL ENDINGS are added to the different tense stems. These distinguish the different persons of the verb. The personal endings of the primary (50) tenses in the active voice are :
