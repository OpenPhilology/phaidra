Paradigms for Imperfect Indicative Active Verbs.

PARADIGMS.

λέγω (Imperfect Active Indicative)
First Person Singular ἔλεγον
Second Person Singular ἔλεγες
Third Person Singular ἔλεγε
Second Person Dual ἐλέγετον
Third Person Dual ἐλεγέτην
First Person Plural ἐλέγομεν
Second Person Plural ἐλέγετε
Third Person Plural ἔλεγον
φέρω (Imperfect Active Indicative)
First Person Singular ἔφερον
Second Person Singular ἔφερες
Third Person Singular ἔφερε
Second Person Dual ἐφέρετον
Third Person Dual ἐφερέτην
First Person Plural ἐφέρομεν
Second Person Plural ἐφέρετε
Third Person Plural ἔφερον
μένω (Imperfect Active Indicative)
First Person Singular ἔμενον
Second Person Singular ἔμενες
Third Person Singular ἔμενε
Second Person Dual ἐμένετον
Third Person Dual ἐμενέτην
First Person Plural ἐμένομεν
Second Person Plural ἐμένετε
Third Person Plural ἔμενον
ἔχω (Imperfect Active Indicative)
First Person Singular εἶχον
Second Person Singular εἶχες
Third Person Singular εἶχε
Second Person Dual εἶχετον
Third Person Dual εἶχέτην
First Person Plural εἴχομεν
Second Person Plural εἴχετε
Third Person Plural εἶχον

Verbs have recessive accent. But, under the general laws, the accent sometimes shifts, as ἔλεγον, ἐλέγομεν, etc., sometimes changes, as εἶχον, I had, εἴχομεν, we had.
